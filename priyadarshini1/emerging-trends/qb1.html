1 Who is known as the father of Artificial Intelligence?
(a) Alan Turing
(b) John McCarthy
(c) Andrew Ng
(d) Geoffrey Hinton
Correct Answer: (b) John McCarthy
2 Which discipline is NOT directly a part of AI core contributors?
(a) Mathematics
(b) Psychology
(c) Astrology
(d) Linguistics
Correct Answer: (c) Astrology
3 What is the ultimate goal of Artificial Intelligence?
(a) Only solve mathematical problems
(b) Mimic human intelligence to solve complex tasks
(c) Operate machines without human intervention
(d) Build hardware devices
Correct Answer: (b) Mimic human intelligence to solve complex tasks
4 Which statement fits "Weak AI" or "Narrow AI"?
(a) Has general human-level intelligence
(b) Excels at multiple tasks simultaneously
(c) Performs dedicated tasks within a limited scope
(d) Can reason like a human
Correct Answer: (c) Performs dedicated tasks within a limited scope
5 What differentiates Super AI from General AI?
(a) Super AI has narrowly focused skills
(b) General AI can exceed human intellectual performance
(c) Super AI surpasses human intelligence in every domain
(d) Super AI has no cognitive abilities
Correct Answer: (c) Super AI surpasses human intelligence in every domain
6 Reactive Machines in AI are characterized by:
(a) The ability to store past experiences
(b) Purely reacting to current inputs without memory
(c) Being self-aware
(d) Understanding human emotions
Correct Answer: (b) Purely reacting to current inputs without memory
7 What is the major focus of Theory of Mind AI?
(a) Data storage
(b) Understanding emotions and beliefs
(c) Performing calculations quickly
(d) Robot mechanics
Correct Answer: (b) Understanding emotions and beliefs
8 Which AI category is still hypothetical and involves consciousness?
(a) Reactive Machines

9 AI helps in healthcare by:

12 Which is an example of supervised learning?

14 Unsupervised learning primarily involves:
(a) Learning from labeled data
(b) Discovering patterns from unlabeled data

15 Reinforcement learning is based on:
(a) Receiving rewards or penalties through trial and error
(b) Grouping data clusters
(c) Identifying keywords

16 Which of the following is NOT an example of ML application?

(a) Support Vector Machines

20 Which field overlaps with AI/ML but is focused on data-driven insights?

(a) Decision Trees

(a) Simple numeric data only

(a) Lack of data

(a) More data and training time

(a) Algorithmic trading

36 What is the output layer's job?
(a) Process input data

(a) Because they are simple to understand
(b) Their internal decision processes are complex and hard to interpret

38 Which method helps neural networks learn from error?

UNIT 2: INTERNET OF THINGS (IoT)
1 IoT stands for ………
(a) Internet of Technology
(b) Intranet of Things
(c) Internet of Things
(d) Information of Things
Correct Answer: (c) Internet of Things
2 What is the Internet of Things (IoT)?
(a) A technology used only for smartphones
(b) A network of interconnected physical devices that exchange data
(c) A type of computer virus
(d) A communication protocol for websites
Correct Answer: (b) A network of interconnected physical devices that exchange data
3 Which of the following is a key characteristic of IoT?
(a) Manual operation
(b) Isolation of devices
(c) Interconnectivity
(d) Lack of automation
Correct Answer: (c) Interconnectivity
4 Which IoT feature enables devices to operate without human involvement?
(a) Automation
(b) Virtualization
(c) Encryption
(d) Translation
Correct Answer: (a) Automation
5 "Things" in IoT refers to ………
(a) only smartphones
(b) any physical object with a sensor and connectivity
(c) website links
(d) only computers
Correct Answer: (b) any physical object with a sensor and connectivity
6 Which communication technology is widely used in IoT?
(a) HDMI
(b) USB
(c) Wi-Fi
(d) VGA
Correct Answer: (c) Wi-Fi
7 Which of the following is an application of IoT?
(a) Social media chatting
(b) Smart home automation
(c) Playing offline games
(d) File compression
Correct Answer: (b) Smart home automation
8 IoT allows devices to collect and share data using ………
(a) Sensors
(b) CDs
(c) Printers
(d) Speakers
Correct Answer: (a) Sensors
9 Which is an advantage of IoT?
(a) Reduced automation
(b) Increased human effort
(c) Real-time monitoring
(d) Less data availability
Correct Answer: (c) Real-time monitoring
10 Which of the following is a limitation of IoT?
(a) High scalability
(b) Security and privacy issues
(c) Increased efficiency
(d) Better data collection
Correct Answer: (b) Security and privacy issues
